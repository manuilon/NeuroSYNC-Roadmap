Where the UK actually stands

This summary draws on an independent SWOT analysis of neuromorphic technology (NMT) in the UK, commissioned by the Netherlands Innovation Network UK and NeuMat and published in October 2025, authored by researchers from Cambridge, UCL, Manchester and Innovate UK. We use it as an evidence base for our roadmap priorities, not as our own claim.
